About club

The Concept

Welcome to the Court.

Clubcourt is a premium padel academy and social hub in Dubai. A place where sport meets lifestyle — designed for players, built for community.

World-class courts, comprehensive wellness amenities, and vibrant social spaces under one roof. Every detail considered. Every experience curated.

The Club

8000 SQM of sport, wellness, and lifestyle. World-class padel courts, a full gym and recovery zone, coworking with court views, and a social lounge — all under one roof in Dubai.

01

The Arena

Up to 16 professional padel courts arranged around a central viewing spine. Show courts with retractable seating for tournaments and events. The heart of the club.

02

The Lifestyle Hub

Gym and fitness zone. Yoga and Pilates studio. Wellness and recovery with cold plunge, sauna, and treatment rooms. Coworking lounge with court views. F&B bar and lounge.

03

The Welcome

Reception and digital check-in. Premium changing rooms. Pro shop with rackets and branded merchandise. Coaching and academy office. Kids activity zone.
